📱 Share via WhatsApp

Share your QR code with customers via WhatsApp. They can scan and order instantly!
💡 How it works:
1. Fill in your vendor details
2. Click "Share via WhatsApp"
3. WhatsApp opens with pre-written message
4. Select recipient and send!

📧 Share via Email

Send QR code details to customers via email with attachments.
💡 What they'll receive:
• Your QR code as attachment
• Shop details (name, price, location)
• Direct ordering link
• Instructions to scan and order

💬 Share via SMS

Send QR link via text message to customer phone numbers.
💡 Preview message:

📱 Scan: [QR Link]

📍 [City]
