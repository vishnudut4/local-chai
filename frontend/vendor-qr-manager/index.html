📖 How to Use QR Codes

💡 Quick Guide:
Generate a unique QR code for your shop. Customers scan it to place orders and get auto-registered!

Step 1: Generate Your QR Code

• Fill in your vendor details (name, mobile, city, price)
• Click "Generate QR Code"
• Download or print the QR code

Step 2: Display the QR Code

• Print and display the QR code at your shop counter
• Put it on your menu board
• Share it with regular customers via WhatsApp

Step 3: Customer Scans QR

• Customer opens camera and scans QR
• Gets redirected to quick order page
• Your shop details auto-filled
• Customer enters name & mobile
• Places order

Step 4: Auto Registration

• Customer is automatically registered in your ledger
• Order is recorded
• Due amount is tracked
• Customer can check their balance anytime

✅ Benefits:
• Faster ordering process
• Automatic customer registration
• No manual data entry
• Reduced mistakes
• Better customer tracking
